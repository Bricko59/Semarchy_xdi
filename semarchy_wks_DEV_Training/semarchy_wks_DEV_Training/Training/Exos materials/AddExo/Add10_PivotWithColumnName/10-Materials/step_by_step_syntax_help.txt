Expression Editor of the filter
-------------------------------
PARAM_COL.PIVOT_COL = true

Expression editor of the mapping fields
---------------------------------------

ID_REF 		-->	ENTRANCE_TABLE.ID_REF
COLUMN_NAME	-->	PARAM_COL.COLUMN_NAME
COLUMN_VALUE	-->	CASE 
     				    WHEN PARAM_COL.COLUMN_NAME='ID_CUSTOMER' THEN CAST(ENTRANCE_TABLE.ID_CUSTOMER AS VARCHAR) 
     				    WHEN PARAM_COL.COLUMN_NAME='LASTNAME' THEN CAST(ENTRANCE_TABLE.LASTNAME AS VARCHAR) 
     				    WHEN PARAM_COL.COLUMN_NAME='FIRSTNAME' THEN CAST(ENTRANCE_TABLE.FIRSTNAME AS VARCHAR)  
     				    WHEN PARAM_COL.COLUMN_NAME='MOBILE' THEN CAST(ENTRANCE_TABLE.MOBILE AS VARCHAR)       
     				    WHEN PARAM_COL.COLUMN_NAME='EMAIL' THEN CAST(ENTRANCE_TABLE.EMAIL AS VARCHAR)        
     				    WHEN PARAM_COL.COLUMN_NAME='BIRTHDATE' THEN CAST(ENTRANCE_TABLE.BIRTHDATE AS VARCHAR)  
			        END
